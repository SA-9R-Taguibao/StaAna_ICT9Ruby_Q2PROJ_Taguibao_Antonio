Recipe for Chicken and Waffles (from Sweet Tea and Thyme)

Ingredients:

Chicken:

  • 8 chicken thighs
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Brine:

  • 480 mL buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika

Batter:

  • 384 g all purpose flour
  • 43 g cornstarch
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

Waffle

  • 2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Chicken

  1. Whisk the ingredients used for the brine in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add the chicken into the mix. Cover the bowl in a plastic wrap to let the chicken marinate for at least an hour, or overnight in the fridge.
  3. In a pot, fill half of it up with vegetable oil, then heat the oil so it can reach 180 C.
  4. Whisk the ingredients you just put on the batter, and once the chicken is done marinating, dredge the chicken in the batter.
  5. Fry the chicken for about 8 to 9 minutes untl golden brown.
  6. Take the chicken out of the oil and place on a baking sheet.

Waffles

  1. Preheat your waffle iron and spray with non stick cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk your flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In a separate medium bowl, beat egg whites with a hand mixer (or a stand mixer) until peaks form.
  4. Whisk the wet and dry parts until most lumps are gone, then using a rubber spatula, gently fold the egg whites.
  5. Pour your mixture into the waffle iron.
