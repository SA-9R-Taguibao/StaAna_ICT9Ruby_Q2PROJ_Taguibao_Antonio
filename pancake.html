Recipe for Pancakes (from Inspired Taste)

Ingredients:

  • 195 g all purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp table salt
  • 295 mL milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 5 tbsp unsaltedb utter
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter, then set aside
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Then, create a bowl in the dry ingredients, and pour in your wet ingredients.
  4. Stir the mixture gently with a fork.
  5. Place a large griddle over medium heat, and brush with the melted butter.
  6. Scoop the batter using a 1/4 cup measuring spoon, and make sure the diameter is 4 inches.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
