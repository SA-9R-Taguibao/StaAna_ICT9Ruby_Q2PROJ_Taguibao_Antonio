Recipe for Scrambled Eggs (from Inspired Taste)

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tbsp butter or olive oil

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter in medium heat.
  2. Crack all 4 eggs into a bowl. Add salt, then whisk. Let it sit for 5 to 10 mins.
  3. When the butter is fully melted, add the egg mix. Use a silicone spatula to make small swirls for 30 seconds, until you see the eggs form thick and very small curds. Then, make sweeps around the pan for 20 seconds until you see larger curds.
  4. Remove from heat when the eggs are softly set and slightly runny. Leave the eggs for a few seconds, then give one final mix.
